2019W1 Study Guide

Study Tips:

  • Follow these 5 steps:
  1. Go through the Learning Goals
  2. Rank the building blocks you're comfortable with from top to bottom. Feeling comfortable means you can answer ANY question in ANY format relating to that building block.
  3.  Do the practice questions on Canvas that relate to each building block
  4.  Once you’re comfortable with the building blocks, take the practice finals in an exam-like environment
  5.  Using the answer sheet grade those exams yourself to see how you did.

  • Take breaks. Study no more than 90 min at a time.
  • Begin studying days in advance. DO NOT cram last minute. It won’t help.

What to Avoid on the Practice Exams:

Course Learning Goals:

[CT BB] use abstraction and decomposition to clarify and simplify the critical pieces of a problem; choose appropriate models and representations to aid in solving the problem; recognize potential shortcomings in a model or solution; explain at a high level how the computer works

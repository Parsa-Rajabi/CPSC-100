1. Give two reasons as to why diversity in computer science is important.

    It allows for:

    • Inventions that suit needs of different minorities. For example, Nicole Torcolini developed electronic braille and various apps that help support different styles of learning.
    • Designs that better suit every individual and can help avoid consequences of poor design. For example, cars designed for men can cause inconveniences for women (e.g., things are slightly out of reach as the average male is taller/has longer limbs than the average female), as well as the life/death matter of fighter planes not made for women.
    • Companies to reach out to more potential customers and recruit more talent.

  2. Give two theories that could point to why there are fewer women than men in computer science.

    • Most women in computer science are introduced to computer science by someone in their life involved in STEM. Not every woman has that opportunity.
    • Young girls are influenced to behave in certain ways and be interested in certain things. For example, look at the difference between lego commercials for girls and boys.

  3. How has the enrolment of women in Computer Science differed from other degrees over the past 40 years?

    Other degrees have seen an increase in female enrolment. However, the numebr of women in Computer Science peaked in the 1980s and has actually declined since then. The current trend seems to indicate that it is on a slow rise.

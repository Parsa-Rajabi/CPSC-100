1. Google often keeps track of your device location. What kinds of advantages and disadvantages would there be to this kind of tracking?

    Note that answers may vary.

    Advantages:

    • It can allow the police to track the previous whereabouts of a missing person or person of interest when investigating crimes.
    • Services like Google Maps will be more accurate when it comes to predicting traffic or reporting on current conditions.
    • City planning services can use this data to see where buildings/services should be placed for maximum convenience.

    Disadvantages:

    • Lack of privacy. By constantly sending your location data, you are effectively allowing your movements to be tracked continuously.
    • You have no control over your own data. If your data is sold, you may receive much more targeted ads. Your data may be used against you in a court of law or in other situations.
